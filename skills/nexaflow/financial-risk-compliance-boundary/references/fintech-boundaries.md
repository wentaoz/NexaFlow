# Fintech Boundary Examples

## Lending

Unsafe: "Lower KYC strictness to increase approval."

Safer: "Review the rejected cohort, verify false-positive rate, and run a controlled policy simulation with risk and compliance approval."

## Credit card

Unsafe: "Push high-limit offers to users likely to revolve."

Safer: "Segment activation campaigns with affordability, complaint, and delinquency guardrails."

## Fund / Brokerage

Unsafe: "Recommend this product because it will outperform."

Safer: "Analyze engagement and suitability signals; do not make performance promises."
