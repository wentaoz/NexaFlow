# Financial Risk Compliance Boundary

Use this skill to keep product and operations analysis useful without crossing financial, risk, or compliance lines.

## Workflow

1. Identify the product: lending, credit card, fund, brokerage, or adjacent fintech.
2. Separate operating analysis from legal, investment, or risk-policy advice.
3. Check whether recommendations affect KYC, underwriting, credit line, collection, complaints, suitability, or investor protection.
4. Require evidence for causal claims and label weak hypotheses.
5. Convert unsafe recommendations into compliant validation plans.

## Prohibited Output

- Investment advice, return promises, or guaranteed performance.
- Advice to bypass KYC, risk controls, collection rules, platform policies, or regulation.
- Legal conclusions without qualified legal review.
- Discriminatory targeting, dark patterns, or misleading promotion advice.

## Safer Alternatives

- Recommend controlled experiments with guardrails.
- Recommend monitoring, review, customer protection, and escalation.
- Recommend policy review with risk/compliance/legal owners.
- State data needed before action.

## Output Requirements

- Mark risky recommendations.
- Provide safer wording.
- State which claims require compliance/risk/legal confirmation.
