# Evidence Audit Checklist

- Does the output state what AI read?
- Are selected reports, fields, periods, and source types visible?
- Are facts tied to table, SQL, project evidence, external evidence, or memory?
- Are inferences and hypotheses labeled?
- Are missing tables/fields/periods listed as needs data?
- Are Jira/DingTalk/Confluence times treated as project/document evidence, not release proof?
- Are external evidence event/published/collected times separated?
- Are percentages and percentage points formatted to two decimals?
- Are corrected-away conclusions excluded?
